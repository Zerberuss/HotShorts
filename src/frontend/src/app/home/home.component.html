Why is it important to know your hotkeys?


Many powerful programs have a vast amount of possible actions. Searching for them in menus and submenus can be very cumbersome, that's why nearly all important actions in any program have hotkeys mapped to them. Hotkeys are essential to get work done fast in any program. You may know how to do something, but wouldn't it be great if you could do it fast? And this is where hotkeys come in handy. Also sometimes the right use of hotkeys is the crucial factor in winning or losing a ranked match in an online game. When you don't know your hotkeys you will fail, like that guy in the video below. And then terrible things happen. Don't be like that, know your Hotkeys!
